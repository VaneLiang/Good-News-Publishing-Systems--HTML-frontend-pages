Six year old finds Fossil in backyard

By jarvis_admin
Post on 26 March 2021
1 Comment
At 108 many might not be up to too much, but for Marion Dawson it’d be hard to say that’s the case – The 108 year old Houston, Renfrewshire resident has received the COVID vaccination not once, but twice. Mrs. Dawson makes history, being the 3rd oldest Scott to complete their rounds of vaccinations for the COVID-19 Epidemic (which through its UK strain especially) has ravaged the economy, life and health of thousands of Britons over the past year. She received her jab at Houston and Killellan Kirk, which is being used by the local GP surgery to deliver vaccinations to the community. Dr. Diane Fisher, who gave Dawson the jab had to say the following “We are so excited to be doing vaccinations for our over-80s, and that our first patient is doing so on her birthday”. Mrs. Dawson in turn is the most senior person in NHS Greater Glasgow & Clyde to be given the vaccine. After receiving the jab the senior had to say the following “I’m glad it’s passed, I never felt a thing. Great for Dawson and her community, the 108 year old native – who never missed a week of church until the pandemic and has lived through two wars and a pandemic herself already, is truly a treasure to her community.
